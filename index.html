Spreadsheet in use: Open GM Agendas Sheet

How to use:

  1. Log in with an ESN Porto Google account;
  2. Pick the desired GM's date;
  3. Press the buttons as they appear: fetch data, etc
  4. If you want to start with just the topics and fill in the rest later:
    1. Press "Insert data available at the start of the GM" and use the result
    2. Return to the page at the end of the meeting, paste the document's link in the text field, and use "Insert data available at the end of the GM"
  5. Press "View Result" to view your template!
